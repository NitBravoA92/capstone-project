Psychological therapy Week in 2023

Psychological Therapy Week is a major mental health event organized by the HappyMind International Foundation that offers different communities around the world the opportunity to receive psychological help, no matter their age, sex, or religion.

The event seeks to help them in relieving their emotional troubles, fears, anxieties, and doubts, educate them on mental and emotional health issues, and give them the tools to live a full and happy life.

Each year, the foundation holds the event in a different country and invites a group of highly qualified mental health specialists from around the world to form part of the group of collaborators who will be giving talks, conferences, and giving individual and group therapy sessions to the attendees.

We have an important group of sponsoring organizations and companies that are committed to improving the mental health of citizens worldwide and getting a better and healthy world.

You can contact us via email for any questions or concerns you have about the Psychological Therapy Week event in 2023!
