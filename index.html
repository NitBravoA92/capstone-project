"Hello, Powerful Minds!"

Psychological Therapy Week Event 2023

In HappyMind Foundation, we want to build a more habitable and happy place through psychology and sociology. We are pleased to invite you to our Psychological assistance day for the entire community, where we will be offering individual and group psychological therapy sessions for individuals of all ages, races, sex, and social strata. We will also be carrying out different social activities so that you can share with other people and release stress and problems. In this opportunity, we will be helping the community of Quebec, Canada.

2023.06.05(MON) ~ 09(WEN)

@McGill University, FSA ULaval and more.

Main Program

A Better World

Welcome talk on human rights and to introduce our collaborating specialists.

Open your mind

Psychologists talk about mental and emotional health. Attendees will draw their emotions on a mural.

Individual therapy

30min session with a collaborating psychologist who deals with the problems that affect your mental health.

Group therapy

Closed meetings. Tell others what affects you and how you deal with it. Listen to others and reflect.

Conference

Our collaborating psychologists talk about the value of life, family, how to achieve full happiness and personal well-being.
